Welcome to HSA Association – GBC

We are honored to welcome you to the official platform of the Hazara Students Association at George Brown College!

Rooted in tradition and driven by community spirit, our association is dedicated to celebrating and sharing the richness of our culture with pride and joy. We are a vibrant family of students and members who come together to honor our heritage through events, art, language, and storytelling.

Join us as we create a space where culture connects us, identity is embraced, and diversity is celebrated.

Who we are?

We are a group of Hazara students at George Brown College who have come together to form the HSA Association. Our goal is to build a supportive and inclusive community that represents our identity, culture, and values on campus.

Through this association, we aim to organize a wide range of cultural, educational, and social programs that not only bring our community closer but also share the beauty of our heritage with others. From cultural nights and language workshops to community gatherings and collaborative events — we are excited to create meaningful experiences for everyone.
